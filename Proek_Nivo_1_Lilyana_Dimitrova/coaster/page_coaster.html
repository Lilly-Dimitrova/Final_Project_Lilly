Mug Coaster

Things you will need

  1. Clothespins
  2. Glue
  3. Paint (if you want)

How to make it

Take the clothespins, rip them apart and discard the metal piece. If you want you can paint them or leave them plain. Arrange them into the desired position or as in the picture, then glue them together and you are done!
